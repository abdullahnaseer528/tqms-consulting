Financial Advisory

Civil Engineering Advisory

Our Civil Engineering team has a robust capability to design, build, operate, and maintain structures & infrastructure that form the built environment of our modern society, including buildings, bridges, highways, water supply systems, environmental protection systems, ports, railroads, dams, flood control works, airports, and many others. Committed to providing high quality services within project budget and schedule constraints, we offer: Bidding Assistance , Cost Estimating , Project Management, Contract & Construction Administration, and LEED (Leadership in Energy & Environmental Design) support.

Electrical Engineering Advisory

Our electrical and electronics engineers typically work alongside our other engineering disciplines as part of a fully integrated service. Our team of electrical engineers and designers apply their deep knowledge of electrical codes and fundamentals coupled with their hands-on experience to design and implement cost-effective, intuitive, robust solutions for electrical challenges. From a short circuit/fault current analysis to an arc flash study, electrical gear upgrades to entire industrial plant electrical power and motor control systems, we have the skill set, knowledge base, and experience to fill your electrical needs, large or small.

Mechanical Engineering Advisory

Mechanical engineering is one of the largest, broadest, and oldest engineering disciplines that uses the principles of energy, materials, and mechanics to design and manufacture machines and devices of all types thereby creating the processes and systems that drive technology and industry.


Our Mechanical engineering section has a robust capability to design, develop, build, and test mechanical and thermal devices, including tools, engines, and machines.

Telecom Engineering Advisory

Our Telecommunication Team has a robust capability of designing and overseeing the installation of telecommunications equipment and facilities, such as complex electronic switching systems, optical fiber cabling, IP networks, and microwave transmission systems. We offer support in all types of telecommunication projects including: public and private wireline and wireless networks; utility operations networks; public safety and first responder communications; IT/OT network security and regulatory compliance; grid modernization; fiber broadband networks; distribution modernization; and smart cities.

Investment & Portfolio Management

Our business consulting team provide a wide range of services to investors and entrepreneurs including, business portfolio analysis, fund raising, JVs, Mergers, Acquisitions, Consolidation, Feasibility Studies, business plan etc. TQMS enables structured and data-driven communication between investors and entrepreneurs. For investors, we enable data-driven portfolio decisions and deliver cost effective portfolio monitoring and reporting. For Investors we provide due diligence to monitor every company in their portfolios with easy comparison and benchmarking.
